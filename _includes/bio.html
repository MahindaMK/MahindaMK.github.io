Short Bio

I am a researcher at IAIK, Graz University of Technology. My main research interests are in the field of public key cryptography with a focus on privacy enhancing authentication primitives and their applications. Before joining IAIK in 2013, I completed both, my master studies (Dipl.-Ing., 2013) and my bachelor studies (BSc, 2011) in Software Development and Business Management at the Graz University of Technology with distinction. Currently, I am involved in various EU funded projects, focusing on privacy preserving identity management as well as secure and privacy preserving cloud computing. In addition to that, I teach courses on modern public key cryptography and system-level programming.
